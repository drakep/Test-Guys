What you need to buy a test guy

You will need Solana to purchase a Test Guy.
‍
Solana can be obtained at a number of crypto exchanges, including:

Coinbase, FTX, Voyager, Binance, and more.

We recommend having .1 SOL to cover the Solana network transaction fee.

How to send sol to phantom

You'll need SOL in your Phantom wallet to get a Test Guy.

To find your Phantom wallet address, please follow the steps we've
outlined below.

1

Open Phantom and press "Receive"

2

Select "Solana"

3

Copy your address

How to buy a test guy

Once minting is available, a "Connect Wallet" button will appear on our minting page.

After clicking the button, a popup from Phantom will appear and prompt you to connect to our website.

Click "Connect." We recommend keeping "Auto-approve transactions" off for the time being.

You can now purchase a Test Guy. Navigate back to our minting page and click the "Mint" button.

After pressing "Mint" you will see one last popup from Phantom asking for your confirmation for the transaction.
