F.A.Q

WHAT IS A TEST GUY

A Test Guy is one of the first 707 animated mp4 NFTs available on Solana.
Canonically, they are humans being experimenting on in the name of helping mankind.

How Can I View My Test Guy?

Head on over to:
‍
NFT Eyez

and input your wallet address.
You can see your Test Guy and his animation there, but don't forget to press play as it is a video.

How Can I Convert Him To a .GIF?

Get the URL from NFTEyez and use a service like Cloud Convert to convert that URL into a .gif.

Cloud Convert

How Many Are There? Will More Be Minted?

There are a total of 707 Test Guy NFTs.
101 different Guys, 7 different animations.
There will NOT be any more of the original Test Guys minted; this contract is immutable.

How Do I Mint a Test Guy?

Test Guys are officially SOLD OUT.
Minting is unavailable.

I Minted a Test Guy, What Am I Supporting?

Please check out our vision for an in-depth answer to that question!

Our Vision

What Can I Do With My Test Guys?

The boiled-down version of the idea behind Test Guys is to use the NFTs in your collection, then populate them in a game we're designing for the community!

How Can I Listen to My MP3 Airdrop?

You can put in your wallet address or link in your wallet to Step Finance and then right click and download and save file. It will be an mp3 you can play. Phantom support for mp3 is coming very soon (as early as next week) and other galleries and wallets are also working on video and audio support.
